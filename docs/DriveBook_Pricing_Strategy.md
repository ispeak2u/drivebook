# DriveBook Pricing Strategy


DriveBook Pricing Strategy

Objective
Create a pricing model that makes it easy for Toronto driving instructors to join DriveBook while generating sustainable revenue.

Phase 1: Launch Strategy

Instructor Pricing
- Free to join
- No setup fee
- No monthly fee
- 10% commission on completed lessons

Student Pricing
- Free to use DriveBook
- Students pay normal lesson fees

Why This Works
- Removes risk for instructors
- Easier instructor acquisition
- Revenue starts immediately from successful bookings

Example
Lesson Fee: CAD $60
DriveBook Commission (10%): CAD $6
Instructor Receives: CAD $54

Phase 2: DriveBook Pro

After:
- 25+ active instructors
- 500+ completed bookings

Free Plan
- No monthly fee
- 10% commission

DriveBook Pro
- CAD $19.99/month
- 5% commission

Competitive Advantages
- Verified reviews
- Online booking
- Automated scheduling
- Cancellation accountability
- Language matching
- Pickup pin locations
- Reminder notifications

Launch Goals
- 25 instructors onboarded
- 100 students registered
- 100 completed bookings

Long-Term Revenue Opportunities
- Instructor subscriptions
- Featured listings
- Road test packages
- Advertising partnerships
- Premium verification

Recommendation
Launch with free instructor signup and a 10% commission.
After traction, introduce DriveBook Pro at CAD $19.99/month with a reduced 5% commission.
