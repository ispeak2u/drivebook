
# DriveBook Competitive Analysis

## Competitors
- Facebook Groups
- Kijiji
- WeChat Communities
- Traditional Driving Schools

## Weaknesses of Existing Solutions
- No verified reviews
- Manual scheduling
- Poor accountability
- No language matching
- No centralized booking

## DriveBook Advantages
- Online booking
- Verified reviews
- Instructor ratings
- Cancellation policies
- Language matching
- Automated reminders

## Strategic Position
Become the trusted marketplace for driving instruction in Toronto.
