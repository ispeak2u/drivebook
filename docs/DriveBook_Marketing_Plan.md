# DriveBook Step-by-Step Marketing Plan

## Purpose
DriveBook is a Toronto-based marketplace that helps students book verified driving instructors on demand, while helping instructors get reliable students without constantly advertising on Facebook, Kijiji, WeChat, or word of mouth.

## Core Positioning

### For Students
**Need to pass your G2 or G test? Book a verified instructor available when you are.**

DriveBook helps students find instructors by availability, rating, language, location, and lesson type.

### For Instructors
**Stop chasing students. Upload your availability and let bookings come to you.**

DriveBook helps instructors manage schedules, reduce cancellations, receive bookings, and build trust through verified reviews.

---

## Phase 1: Validate Instructor Demand First

### Goal
Get 10-25 Toronto-based driving instructors interested before heavy marketing spend.

### Why This Comes First
A marketplace needs supply before demand. If students visit DriveBook and there are no instructors, the app feels empty. Instructors are the first domino.

### Actions
1. Build a simple instructor waitlist form.
2. Contact independent driving instructors manually.
3. Search Facebook, Kijiji, WeChat, Google Maps, and local driving school listings.
4. Message instructors with a simple pitch.
5. Track responses in a spreadsheet or CRM.

### Instructor Pitch
> DriveBook helps Toronto driving instructors get booked without constantly posting ads or chasing students. You upload your availability, students book available lesson times, and the platform manages reminders, reviews, cancellations, and scheduling.

### Target Outcome
- 10 instructors interested
- 5 instructors willing to test the MVP
- Feedback on pricing, scheduling, cancellations, and language filters

---

## Phase 2: Build the Student Waitlist

### Goal
Create demand before the app fully launches.

### Student Landing Page Message
> Failed your G2 or G test? Need lessons before your road test? Find verified Toronto driving instructors by schedule, rating, language, and pickup location.

### Waitlist Fields
- Name
- Email
- Phone number
- City/neighborhood
- Preferred language
- Test type: G2, G, beginner, refresher
- Target test date

### Student Hooks
- Book lessons on demand
- Choose instructors by language
- Drop a pickup pin
- See ratings and reviews
- Avoid endless texting and unreliable scheduling

### Target Outcome
- 100 student waitlist signups
- Identify most requested languages
- Identify top neighborhoods
- Identify most common lesson needs

## Phase 4: Content and Clipping Network Strategy

### What a Clipping Network Means for DriveBook
A clipping network is a group of short-form content channels, creators, affiliates, or automated posting workflows that continuously publish useful clips and posts that drive traffic to DriveBook.

### Do Not Market Only the App
Market the problems students already care about.

### Content Topics for Students
- 3 reasons people fail the G2 test
- How many lessons should you take before your G test?
- Common parallel parking mistakes
- What to practice before your road test
- Best tips for nervous drivers
- What happens if you fail your G2?
- How to find a driving instructor in Toronto
- Why language matters when learning to drive

### Content Topics for Instructors
- Tired of students cancelling last minute?
- Stop posting driving lesson ads every week
- How instructors can fill empty lesson slots
- Why verified reviews help instructors win more students
- How DriveBook helps instructors manage bookings

### Channels
- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts
- Facebook Reels
- Facebook Groups
- Reddit
- WeChat
- WhatsApp groups
- LinkedIn for instructor/business credibility

### Content Rule
Each clip should end with one simple call to action:

For students:
> Join the DriveBook waitlist to find verified Toronto driving instructors.

For instructors:
> Join DriveBook as a founding instructor.

## Phase 7: Launch Plan

### Soft Launch Requirements
Before launch, aim for:
- 10-25 instructors
- 100+ student waitlist signups
- 3-5 language categories covered
- Basic booking flow working
- Instructor availability working
- Review/rating flow planned or built
- Cancellation policy drafted

### First Launch Area
Start with one or two strong areas:
- Scarborough
- North York
- Brampton
- Mississauga
- Toronto core

Do not try to cover all of Ontario first.

## First 30-Day Execution Plan

### Week 1
- Create landing page copy
- Create instructor waitlist form
- Create student waitlist form
- Build outreach spreadsheet
- Draft instructor outreach messages

### Week 2
- Contact 50 instructors
- Post in local Facebook/Kijiji-style communities where allowed
- Create first 10 short-form content ideas
- Interview 5 students who recently failed or booked a road test

### Week 3
- Contact another 50 instructors
- Start posting 1-2 short clips per day
- Create language-based landing page drafts
- Collect feedback from interested instructors

### Week 4
- Confirm first 10 founding instructors
- Confirm top student pain points
- Finalize MVP marketing copy
- Prepare soft launch campaign
