# DriveBook Investor One-Pager

## Company Overview

DriveBook is a marketplace that connects driving students with verified driving instructors through a simple, accountable, and convenient booking platform.

Initial launch market: Toronto, Ontario.

---

## The Problem

Students struggle to find trusted instructors.

Current options:
- Friends and referrals
- Facebook groups
- Kijiji
- WeChat communities

Problems:
- No accountability
- Difficult scheduling
- Limited reviews
- Poor user experience

Instructors face:
- Expensive advertising
- Constant student acquisition efforts
- Manual scheduling
- No-shows and cancellations
- Administrative headaches

---

## The Solution

DriveBook provides:

- Instructor discovery
- Online booking
- Verified reviews
- Language matching
- Availability management
- Cancellation accountability
- Notifications and reminders
- Pickup location selection

Students can book lessons on demand.

Instructors receive qualified leads and booking management tools.

---

## Market Opportunity

Toronto is one of North America's most multicultural cities.

Strong demand exists among:
- New drivers
- International newcomers
- Students preparing for G2 and G road tests

Language matching creates a unique competitive advantage.

---

## Revenue Model

Phase 1:
- Free instructor signup
- 10% commission on completed lessons

Phase 2:
- DriveBook Pro
- CAD $19.99/month
- Reduced 5% commission

Future:
- Featured listings
- Premium verification
- Road test packages
- Partnerships

---

## Competitive Advantage

- Marketplace focus
- Accountability system
- Language-specific instructor matching
- Verified reviews
- Automated booking workflow
- Instructor-focused lead generation

---

## Launch Strategy

1. Recruit 25 Toronto instructors
2. Build student waitlist
3. Launch content clipping network
4. Generate first 100 completed bookings
5. Introduce premium plans

---

## 12-Month Goals

- 100+ instructors
- 1,000+ students
- 5,000+ completed bookings
- Recurring subscription revenue
- Expansion across Ontario

---

## Vision

Become the leading driving instructor marketplace in Canada by making it easier for students to find trusted instructors and easier for instructors to grow their business.
