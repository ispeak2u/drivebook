# DriveBook Go-To-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

DriveBook is a marketplace connecting Toronto driving students with verified driving instructors.

The initial goal is to build supply (instructors) before aggressively scaling demand (students).

---

## Phase 1: Build Instructor Supply

### Goal
Recruit 25 qualified Toronto-area driving instructors.

### Sources
- Facebook groups
- Kijiji driving lesson ads
- WeChat communities
- Independent instructors
- Driving schools

### Value Proposition
- Stop chasing students
- Receive bookings automatically
- Manage availability in one place
- Reduce no-shows and cancellations
- Build reputation through verified reviews

### KPI
- 25 instructor signups
- 15 approved instructors
- 10 active instructors

---

## Phase 2: Student Waitlist

### Goal
Build a database of prospective students before launch.

### Landing Page
Collect:
- Name
- Email
- Phone
- Preferred language
- G2 or G test preparation

### KPI
- 100 waitlist registrations

---

## Phase 3: Content and Clipping Network

### Objective
Create educational driving content that attracts students.

### Content Topics
- How to pass your G2 test
- Common reasons students fail
- Parallel parking tips
- Road test preparation
- New driver advice

### Distribution Channels
- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts
- Facebook Reels
- WeChat

### Call To Action
Book a verified instructor through DriveBook.
