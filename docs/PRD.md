# Product Requirements Document

Placeholder for the DriveBook product requirements.

## Product Summary

DriveBook is a driving instructor marketplace for student discovery, instructor onboarding, availability, bookings, payments, reviews, notifications, and admin operations.

## MVP Scope

- Student and instructor registration.
- Instructor profile and approval workflow.
- Availability and booking flow.
- Payment and subscription foundation.
- Lesson completion, ratings, disputes, and notifications.
- Admin dashboard workflows.
